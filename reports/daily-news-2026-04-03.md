The E2B and E4B models are also the foundation for Gemini Nano 4, Google’s next-generation on-device model for Android, which will arrive on consumer devices later this year. Gemma has accumulated more than 400 million downloads and over 100,000 community-created variants since its first release, a figure Google points to as evidence of developer adoption at scale. Gemma 4 is available immediately on Hugging Face, Kaggle, and Ollama, with the 31B and 26B models accessible via Google AI Studio and the edge models via AI Edge Gallery.
