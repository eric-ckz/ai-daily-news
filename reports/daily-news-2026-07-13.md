"We're excited for this next chapter under Mia Glaese's leadership across research and safety." The reorganization comes as OpenAI reconsiders the relationship between its research and safety efforts. "Safety — the practice of enabling AI's positive impacts by mitigating the negative ones — is thus core to our mission," the company says. OpenAI, however, has a poor track record of retaining its top safety leaders, and some departing employees have publicly questioned its commitment to that work.
