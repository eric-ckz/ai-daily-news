The platform enables health systems to interact more directly with payers and life sciences organizations via clinical workflows. Abridge said the platform will help clinicians prepare for visits, support them during the conversation, and reduce administrative tasks after care is delivered. It is designed to help coordinate financial, clinical, and decision-making throughout care. The company is also expanding to nurses, enabling ambient AI to capture nurse-patient conversations and automatically generate documents in the EHR based on those discussions. "This next chapter brings trusted intelligence into the most important moment in medicine: a clinician caring for a patient," Dr. Shiv Rao, CEO and cofounder of Abridge, said in a statement.
