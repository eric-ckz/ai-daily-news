[##### **Related Story** Ubuntu 26.04 (Linux) OS Now Has Heavier PC Requirements Than Microsoft Windows 11](https://wccftech.com/ubuntu-26-04-linux-os-heavier-pc-requirements-vs-microsoft-windows-11/) Based on the details shared by the user, the motherboard is still an early engineering sample, and based on the design, it looks definitely like a laptop solution, though the user states that this compact design is also suitable for compact yet powerful tablets.    - 摘要：Q2 2026 # PitchBook Analyst Note: Agentic AI: The Evolution to Autonomous Systems: Part I ### Agentic AI is rewriting the software value chain Agentic AI is rapidly moving from experimentation to deployment, and capital is following. In 2025 alone, VC-backed agentic AI companies raised $24.2 billion across 1,311 deals, representing nearly 73% of their cumulative VC deal value between 2015 and 2024. This surge reflects a structural shift in enterprise adoption: away from seat-based software-as-a-service models and toward systems that execute end-to-end workflows, tying value directly to outcomes rather than usage. This transition is reshaping how software is built, priced, and monetized. Investment is concentrating heavily in IT-centric verticals such as cybersecurity, developer tooling, and enterprise productivity, where deployment is fastest and return on investment is measurable. North America continues to dominate, capturing 95.6% of the combined post-money valuations of agentic AI companies, driven by its deep venture ecosystem and proximity to core AI infrastructure. Exit dynamics reinforce a bifurcated market. Platform-scale companies controlling orchestration layers are positioned for outsized outcomes, while application-layer startups are increasingly “built to be bought,” with M&A remaining the primary exit pathway. As agentic systems evolve into multiagent architectures capable of managing entire workflows, the competitive edge is shifting toward orchestration, integration, and system-level control, signaling a
