Shipments to China fetched more than $104 billion for 15 critical US companies in the semiconductor space, including Nvidia, when their revenues for the most recent fiscal year were combined. The previous year's increase was largely attributable to a surge in smartphone sales, after the release of new 5G handsets. Smaller units selling digital power and automotive technologies also performed well.
